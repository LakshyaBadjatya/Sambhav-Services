📋 1. Introduction

Welcome to Sambhav Services ("the App"), a business management application developed by Lakshya Business Solutions ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App. By using Sambhav Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

This app is intended for authorized business staff only. It is not a public consumer application. Access is restricted to invited users with verified mobile numbers.

📊 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Data Type What We Collect Purpose
Identity Full name, mobile number Account creation and authentication
Device Phone State SIM card presence, device IMEI (read-only) Security verification – detect unauthorized SIM changes
Business Data Invoices, challans, client records, task records Core app functionality
Usage Data Actions performed within the app (e.g. invoice created, packed, delivered) Audit trail and timeline history
Notifications Push notification tokens Send real-time business alerts to staff

📱 3. Phone State & SIM Access

The App requests the READ_PHONE_STATE and READ_PHONE_NUMBERS permissions on Android for the following security purpose:

  • SIM Change Detection: To verify that the SIM card associated with a registered account has not been swapped or replaced by an unauthorized person. If a SIM change is detected, the user may be required to re-authenticate.
Important: We do NOT use this permission to track your location, read call logs, intercept SMS messages, or collect your phone number automatically without consent. The SIM state data is used exclusively for account security and is never shared with third parties.

You may deny this permission; however, some security features may be limited as a result.

⚙️ 4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticate and authorize staff members to access the app
  • Enable core business features: invoice tracking, client management, task management
  • Maintain an audit trail of actions for business accountability
  • Detect unauthorized account access via SIM change detection
  • Send push notifications about invoice and task updates
  • Allow administrators to manage staff accounts and permissions

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside of the business operations described above.

🔒 5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore Database and Firebase Authentication), which is operated by Google LLC and complies with industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

  • Authentication is handled via Firebase Phone Authentication (OTP-based)
  • Database access is restricted by Firestore Security Rules
  • Only authenticated and authorized users can read or write business data
  • Admin users have additional privileges managed within the app

While we take every reasonable measure to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We encourage users to keep their mobile numbers and accounts private.

🤝 7. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within the business: Staff data is visible to authorized administrators of the same business account
  • Legal obligations: If required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Service providers: Firebase/Google as described above, solely to operate the app

🗓️ 8. Data Retention

Business data (invoices, clients, tasks) is retained for as long as the business account remains active. Staff account data is retained until deleted by an administrator.

If you wish to delete your data, please contact us at the email below and we will process your request within 30 days.

👶 9. Children's Privacy

Sambhav Services is a business application intended for adults (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the section below.
